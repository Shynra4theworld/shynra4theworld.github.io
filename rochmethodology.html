Roch Methodology

Our objective as an organization is to deliver the mass development of a specialized power plant over the underlying surface of continents over the world for empowering industries and ecosystems in order to mitigate the influence of Climate Change from the energy industry as well as reshaping mining operations. 

A Roch Plant is the revolutionary design for scaling clean and sustainable electricity over an area without the environmentally invasive approaches of conventional energy technologies, hence its subterranean development.

The Roch Methodology was designed with the framework of mining, hydro and geothermal operations in synchrony. The system emulates the superior value proposition of Hydro dams and hydrothermal resources without their major negative externalities while offering unique value propositions such as the availability for development within a mining operation as our CVO intended originally after his education in Mining Engineering.

A Roch plant has been calculated to be affordable, clean and a reliable design, over other sources, for mitigating the GHG, preserving ecosystems while supporting modern society and economies to grow over an extended period of time for enhancing BOTH industries in a meaningful way.
