HoW IT Began

Our story began with the awareness of the sustainable issues surrounding the Mining industry. The requirement of resources, energy, and time in order to create an economical reserve while mitigating the social and environmental risks surrounding the operation from the beginning to the end of its life.

Mining has been a critical enabler for providing the raw resources, whether gold or potash, for modern and post-civilization to advance. Humankind could not have progressed without the raw resources, such as the stone from the Stone age, the bronze from the Bronze age, and the Industrial, Information and future ages which have all required an amplitude of various minerals over the years for our collaborative growth as a species. 

Yet, each mine has a limited amount of resources to offer onto the global market which leaves it to its inevitable conclusion. The industry has professional standards in place for developing a Mine Reclamation proposal for restoring the land that has been mined to a natural or economically usable state.

On the other hand, this environmental due diligence within the Mining Industry has recently come to surface onto the Energy Industry on a chaotic magnitude for restoring Earth's climate system to post Industrial levels because of our environmental negligence. The Energy Industry is another critical sector for enabling civilization has directly and progressively contributed to civilization advancements with the inexpensive rates of energy prices offered over the years through primarily fossil fuels. In short, Advance civilization is based on advances in energy. However, we have reason to fear that our advances are overall incremental for the storm that is gaining momentum.

Our journey is to guide and establish an advancement to civilization through the development of the Roch Methodology into the energy industry, and additionally into the mining industry. For providing sustainability towards the industries which ensures environmental protection that goes beyond the value delivered towards them.
