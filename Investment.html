Investment

The overall future proposal consists of 4 stages for the development of a Roch Plant in order to hone the accuracy of establishing a profitable operation for future development. Each Project must meet a focused criterion to be considered and reported to external influences of the operation such as Regulators, System Operators, Governmental Jurisdiction, etc. in respecting the stage of development for certifying a certainty of success. The separate stage and their categorization of development are as specified

Stage 1 Resource Procurement and Identification
Stage 2 Resource Exploration and Confirmation
Stage 3 Permitting and Initial Development
Stage 4 Resource Production and Power Plant Construction

The transition in approvals from the evaluations verifies the reliability of a Roch Plant intended for certifying a profitable speculation.
Stage 1 and Stage 2 are primary stages of investment for expansion used for strengthening the foundation of investment for the development of the closing stages, Stage 3 and 4, for the full production of a prospected plant.

Given the specialty and immense value attached to the Roch Methodology, a project assessment is linked to the site specifics of the operation; geology, surrounding geography, the category of supply such as Industry, Business, or Community, yearly demand and permanence of the operation. But the value to be returned is of great importance on the investment which goes beyond views.
